🎯 या सर्वेक्षणाचा उद्देश / Why We Are Conducting This Survey

Xtron Engineering Services Pvt. Ltd. is conducting this survey to design and deliver free / subsidised Seminars & Workshops for Indian MSME manufacturers — helping small factories upgrade their operations, quality, compliance, and digital capabilities to match global standards.

आम्ही हे सर्वेक्षण भारतातील MSME उत्पादकांसाठी मोफत / अनुदानित Seminars आणि Workshops तयार करण्यासाठी घेत आहोत — जेणेकरून छोट्या कारखान्यांना Operations, Quality, Compliance आणि Digital Transformation मध्ये जागतिक दर्जा गाठता येईल.

आम्ही काय करणार / What Xtron Will Do With Your Input

  • Identify the most pressing challenges faced by Indian MSME manufacturers across sectors
  • Design targeted Seminars & Workshops on topics like Digital Tool adoption, GST compliance, quality systems (ISO/BIS), supply chain optimisation, and export readiness
  • Share aggregated findings with industry associations, MSME clusters, and government bodies to advocate for better support programmes

📌 तुमचा अभिप्राय थेट पुढील Seminar & Workshop च्या विषयांवर परिणाम करेल. / Your feedback will directly shape the topics and format of upcoming MSME Seminars & Workshops.

कोणता डेटा घेतला जातो / Data Collected

  • Business firmographic details — sector, employee size, annual turnover range
  • Current operational practices and technology stack
  • Challenges in Finance, Production, Quality, Supply Chain, HR, and Sales
  • Feature & training preferences for seminars and digital tools
  • Your role within the organisation
  • Your name and mobile phone number — collected at the start of the survey for follow-up seminar invitations and to send you details of upcoming Xtron Workshops

Note: Your name and mobile number are collected for seminar/workshop communication only. All other responses remain anonymous in aggregate analysis. You may request deletion at any time by writing to legal@xtronengineering.in.

Other personally identifiable information — name, Aadhaar, PAN, mobile number, or exact address — is collected unless you voluntarily write it in an open-text field.

डेटा कसा वापरला जाईल / How Your Data Is Used

  • Seminar & Workshop curriculum design — content tailored to real MSME pain points
  • Anonymised aggregate reports shared with industry bodies and government MSME schemes
  • Technology and product development — Your aggregated, anonymised responses may be used to research, design, develop, test, and improve software-based tools, services, and technology products intended to support MSME manufacturing operations. This includes the development of digital platforms, operational management systems, and data-driven solutions for the manufacturing sector. No individual respondent shall be identifiable in any such development output, and all such use shall remain within the scope of improving services for the Indian MSME ecosystem.
  • Data is never sold to any third party or used for commercial advertising

आपले अधिकार / Your Rights under DPDP Act, 2023

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India) you have the right to:

  • Access — Request a summary of any personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure — Request deletion of your data at any time
  • Withdraw Consent — Withdraw this consent at any time without affecting prior processing
  • Grievance Redressal — Raise a complaint with our Data Protection Officer or the Data Protection Board of India
  • Nominate a representative — In case of incapacity, you may nominate someone to exercise these rights

डेटा सुरक्षा आणि कालावधी / Security & Retention

  • Responses stored securely and retained for 24 months from the date of collection for research and programme design purposes
  • All data processed within India in compliance with applicable laws
  • Access restricted to authorised Xtron team members only
  • No data shared with advertisers, data brokers, or any commercial third party
